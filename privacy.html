Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Hoops Ledger is a service operated by Hooporia Institute Inc., a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving transparency, accountability, and development in youth basketball programs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, protect, and use information when you use Hoops Ledger.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Enter

Coaches, administrators, and authorized users may enter:

  • Income and expense entries
  • Team names
  • League names
  • Program names
  • Notes related to transactions

We do not require personal information to use Hoops Ledger.

B. PIN-Based Access

Programs access the system using a secure PIN assigned by Hooporia Institute Inc. We do not store the PIN itself — only a one-way encrypted hash.

C. Technical Information

We may automatically collect limited technical data such as:

  • Browser type
  • IP address
  • Device type
  • Error logs

This information helps maintain platform stability and security.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Enable program-level financial tracking
  • Generate summaries, charts, and CSV reports
  • Maintain secure, PIN-protected spaces
  • Improve reliability and performance

3. How We Protect Information

Your program data is isolated. In multi-PIN mode, each program can only see its own entries.

We use:

  • Encrypted PIN hashing
  • Secure storage practices
  • Access-controlled environment variables
  • Regular internal audits

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your information with external advertisers, data brokers, or third-party marketing systems.

We may share information only when required by:

  • Applicable law
  • Valid legal process (“court order”)
  • Safety, misuse, or fraud investigations

5. Data Retention

Financial entries are retained for program transparency and historical recordkeeping unless a program formally requests deletion.

6. Youth Privacy

Hoops Ledger does not collect personal information about minors. Data is intentionally scoped to programs and financial entries, not individuals.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or regulatory requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.
